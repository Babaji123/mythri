Our Profile - Mythri Hospital was established in 1998 as a multi speciality hosptial aimed at bringing the health care costs within the reach of the common man. It is started as a 40 Bed hospital at its Ameerpet Center, Hyderabad, India. Since then it has grown up into a   Super Speciality   Center with   200  beds along with the  latest equipment. Mythri group has corporate health tieup with more than 70 organisations in both private and public sectors.The group is expanding with another 150 Beds Hospital at Main Road, Chanda Nagar which is situated on Mumbai Highway

Our History - Mythri Hospital was started in 1998 by Dr. J.Rama Chandra Rao MBBS. DGS, Dr. J. Lakshmi, MBBS. DGO who has been practicing Surgery and Gynaecology respectively for the last 30 years along with Dr.PrakashB.Vegunta MS,FRCS. & Dr. Madhavi MRCOG who returned to India after getting trained in UK. Dr. Kishore MBBS, MS ( Ortho ) and Dr. Y Haritha MD DGO, who got trained extensively in their respective fields in UK, also joined the organisation subsequently.
