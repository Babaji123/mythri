Our Profile

Mythri Hospital was established in 1998 as a multi speciality hosptial aimed at bringing the health care costs within the reach of the common man. It is started as a 40 Bed hospital at its Ameerpet Center, Hyderabad, India. Since then it has grown up into a Super Speciality Center with 200 beds along with the latest equipment. Mythri group has corporate health tieup with more than 70 organisations in both private and public sectors.The group is expanding with another 150 Beds Hospital at Main Road, Chanda Nagar which is situated on Mumbai Highway
